Frequently Asked Questions

Can I try the hand gesture recognition system?

Yes! We host demo sessions every Friday at 3:00 PM in the Computer Science Building, Room 302. You're welcome to come and try our system firsthand. No registration required.

Is the code for this project open source?

Yes, our project is open source and available on GitHub. We've released it under the MIT license. Feel free to fork, modify, and use it for your own projects. We appreciate attribution and would love to hear about how you're using it!

What hardware requirements are needed to run the system?

Our system runs on most modern computers with a webcam. For optimal performance, we recommend:

  • CPU: Intel i5 or equivalent (or better)
  • RAM: 8GB minimum
  • Camera: 720p webcam or better
  • GPU: While not required, a dedicated GPU will significantly improve performance
Can I collaborate with your team?

We're always open to collaborations! Whether you're a researcher, student, or industry professional, we welcome opportunities to work together. Please reach out via email with details about your interests and how you'd like to collaborate.
